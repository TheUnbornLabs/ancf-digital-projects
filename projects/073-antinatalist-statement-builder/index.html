PROJECT 073

Antinatalist Statement Builder

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate

Draft a measured statement of an antinatalist view — one that argues an idea while clearly respecting all people. Pick a basis and tone, then refine it in your own words.

1. Draft your statement

Your statement will appear here.

2. Make it yours

3. Keep it honest and kind

Argue ideas, never people's worth

The statement is about the ethics of creating life — never about the value of anyone already living, including children and parents. Keep that line bright.

Name your basis clearly

Saying "I ground this in consent" or "in reducing suffering" lets readers engage the actual reasoning instead of a caricature.

Invite good-faith disagreement

Ending with openness ("I welcome disagreement offered in good faith") models the respect you're asking for.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

PROJECT 068

Population Growth Visual Toy

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~5 min

A simple toy for an idea that surprises almost everyone: how fast exponential growth compounds. Slide the inputs and watch the curve. It's pure maths — a neutral visual, with no claim about any country or community.

A maths demonstration only. It says nothing about whether populations should grow or shrink, and never targets any group. Real demographics are far more complex than a single rate.

What you will learn

  • How a steady percentage growth compounds over time.
  • What "doubling time" means.
  • Why exponential curves feel slow, then sudden.

Key takeaway

Exponential curves are famously unintuitive — they creep, then leap. Seeing that clearly is useful for thinking about many things; it implies nothing about what anyone should do.

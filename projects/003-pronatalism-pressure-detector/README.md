# Pronatalism Pressure Detector

*Project 003 · Category: Society · Difficulty: Intermediate*

A 360-degree interactive toolkit for understanding and handling pronatalist pressure — the
social assumption that everyone should have children.

Includes nine modules:
1. **Understand it** — the four levels (interpersonal, cultural, institutional, internalized),
   why pronatalism exists, benevolent vs hostile pressure, and a short history
   (Hollingworth 1916, Russo's "motherhood mandate" 1976, the "biological clock" 1978).
2. **Pressure detector** — paste a message; it highlights pronatalist patterns, decodes the
   assumption beneath each, gauges curiosity vs pressure vs coercion, and suggests replies.
3. **Field guide** — a searchable, filterable library of common lines, each decoded with
   gentle/firm/boundary responses.
4. **Pressure profile** — rate ten sources; see a radar chart, severity, and tailored guidance.
5. **Pressure vs coercion** — a private safety check that flags reproductive coercion.
6. **Boundary composer** — generate a tailored response by relationship, angle, tone, and length.
7. **Scenario simulator** — rehearse real conversations and see likely outcomes plus coaching.
8. **Quick check** — a seven-question quiz.
9. **Reflection** — private prompts saved on-device.

All logic is vanilla JS using the shared `../../ancf-ui.js` toolkit. Nothing is uploaded;
reflections and ratings are stored only in the browser.

> This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or
> mental health advice. If you are experiencing reproductive coercion or feel unsafe, consider
> reaching out to a trusted person or a qualified professional.

PROJECT 080

Community Newsletter Template

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner

A reusable newsletter skeleton for a community group. Pick a cadence and focus, generate a template, then fill it in and copy it into your email tool. A good newsletter is short, kind, and easy to skim.

1. Build your template

2. Fill it in

3. Tips

Lead with one thing

Put the single most useful item up top. Busy readers skim; reward the skim.

Keep it kind and safe

Reaffirm the group's values, and add disclaimers for anything resembling medical, legal, or financial advice.

Always include a way in

End with how to reply, join, or get help. A newsletter is a conversation, not a broadcast.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

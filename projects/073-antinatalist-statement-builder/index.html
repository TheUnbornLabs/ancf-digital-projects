PROJECT 073

Antinatalist Statement Builder

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~7 min

Draft a clear, careful statement of an antinatalist view — one that argues the idea and never demeans people. Pick the basis and the tone, and a built-in respect line keeps it from tipping into hostility. Edit freely.

This tool builds non-hateful statements only. It never insults parents, children, families, or any group, and never encourages harm. Argue the ethics; respect the people.

What you will learn

  • How to state a contested view without contempt.
  • How different bases (consent, suffering, autonomy, ecology) read.
  • How a respect line keeps a strong claim civil.

Key takeaway

A strong view stated with respect persuades more and harms no one. Argue the ethics clearly, keep the people in view, and your statement stays both bold and kind.

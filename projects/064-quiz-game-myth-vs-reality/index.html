Key takeaway

Under time pressure, you answer from instinct, not research — which is exactly how most "common knowledge" gets absorbed in the first place. The level you reached says less than the statements that caught you off guard. Whatever your score, noticing where your snap judgment and the explanation disagreed is the actual point.

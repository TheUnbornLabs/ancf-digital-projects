30-Day Reading Challenge

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~5 min/day

One small prompt a day for thirty days, to build a considered view at a gentle pace. Tick each day as you go; skip and return freely. Your progress saves on this device.

What you will learn

  • A little, consistently, beats a lot, once.
  • How daily prompts build understanding over a month.
  • How to track a habit without pressure.

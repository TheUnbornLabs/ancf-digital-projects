PROJECT 023

Ethics Flashcards

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~8 min

Twelve flip-cards for the key terms in birth ethics and reproductive autonomy. Tap a card (or press Enter) to reveal the definition, mark the ones you know, and watch your progress grow. Great for solo study or a study circle.

What you will learn

  • Definitions of the core vocabulary: consent, asymmetry, autonomy, and more.
  • The difference between easily confused terms (childfree vs childless; actual vs proxy consent).
  • Which terms you've mastered and which to revisit (a progress meter).
  • How to self-test until the words feel natural.

Key takeaway

Clear words make for clear thinking. Once these terms feel natural, the bigger debates become far easier to follow — and to join with care and precision.

PROJECT 068

Population Growth Visual Toy

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · interactive toy

A simple toy for an idea that surprises almost everyone: how fast exponential growth compounds. Slide the starting size, the yearly growth rate, and the number of years, and watch the curve climb. It's pure maths — a way to build intuition. It implies nothing about whether anyone should, or shouldn't, have children.

Key takeaway

Exponential growth is famously hard to picture — small rates produce startling totals given enough time, and a tiny change in the rate changes everything. That's worth understanding clearly, in either direction. But a curve is just a curve: it describes maths, not morality, and decides nothing about any individual's choices.

Key takeaway

The thread running through all hundred of these projects is a single, hopeful idea: a world where having children, or not, is a free and supported choice for everyone — no coercion, no shame, no assumption. Naming that future in your own words is how you start to build it. Keep your board close, and add to it as your vision grows.

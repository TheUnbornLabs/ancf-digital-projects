PROJECT 071

Personal Manifesto Builder

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~8 min

Put your choices into words you can stand on. Answer four prompts and assemble a short, dignified manifesto — for yourself, a journal, or a quiet reminder on a hard day. Edit it freely.

What you will learn

  • How to name your values clearly.
  • How to state what you're building, not just opting out of.
  • How to put a boundary and a hope into your own words.

1. Answer the prompts

2. Quick check

Make them steadier and clearer

Key takeaway

A manifesto isn't a wall against others — it's a compass for you. Name what you value and what you're building, and the boundaries take care of themselves.

If it gets too much

You're allowed to leave early, go quiet, or take a walk. Protecting your peace is not rude — it's how you stay kind under pressure. A boundary you can keep beats a perfect comeback you can't.

If family pressure is affecting your wellbeing more deeply, consider talking it through with someone you trust or a qualified professional. You deserve support.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

PROJECT 069

Resource Allocation Simulator

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · interactive toy

A simple toy showing how a fixed pool divides per person as a household grows. It's just division — a way to picture trade-offs. It implies nothing about an ideal family size, and real households share, pool, and economize in ways plain division ignores.

Key takeaway

Divide a fixed pool among more people and each share shrinks — that part is simple arithmetic. But real life isn't simple division: people share rooms, pool incomes, pass down clothes, and value things money can't measure. Use this to picture one trade-off clearly, not to settle anything about how anyone should live.

PROJECT 078

Video Script Generator

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~6 min

Draft a short, respectful explainer-video script: a hook, a clear body, and a kind call-to-action. Pick a topic and length, then edit to your voice. Built to inform, never to provoke.

What you will learn

  • The shape of a watchable explainer: hook → body → CTA.
  • Why the first five seconds matter most.
  • How to close with an invitation, not a lecture.

1. Build a script

2. Quick check

1. The most important part of an explainer is…
The first few seconds (the hook)
The credits
The length

2. A good CTA is…
A lecture
A kind invitation
A demand

Key takeaway

Hook them early, make one clear point, and end with a warm invitation. A respectful explainer earns attention without demanding it.

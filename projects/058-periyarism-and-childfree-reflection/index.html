PROJECT 058

Periyarism and Childfree Reflection

Society · Difficulty: Advanced

An educational reflection connecting rationalist, self-respect movements with reproductive autonomy — presented respectfully and without speaking for any community.

Self-respect and choice

Rationalist self-respect traditions emphasise dignity and freedom from imposed roles, which resonates with reproductive autonomy.

Questioning custom

Such traditions encourage examining customs critically while respecting people — a model for these debates.

Care with representation

These notes don’t claim any movement endorses antinatalism; they explore shared values like autonomy and reason.

Reflection prompt

Which inherited customs have you chosen to keep, and which to question?

PROJECT 074

Instagram Carousel Text Builder

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~6 min

Generate the text for a five-slide educational carousel — a hook, three clear points, and a kind closing. Pick a topic, then copy the slides. Respectful by design, and light on hashtags.

What you will learn

  • The shape of a strong carousel: hook → points → close.
  • How to make each slide one clear idea.
  • How to end with warmth, not a lecture.

2. Quick check

2. Each middle slide works best with…
One clear idea

Key takeaway

Hook, three clear points, kind close. One idea per slide keeps a carousel readable — and a warm ending invites people in rather than pushing them away.

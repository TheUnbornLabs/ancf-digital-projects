Mini Car Race Game: Escape Pronatalist Pressure

Games · Difficulty: Beginner

Steer your car between three lanes and slip past the deep-red "pressure" blocks. It's a playful metaphor for dodging unwanted questions — no people involved, and nothing to lose but a high score.

Controls: the ◀ / ▶ buttons below, the arrow keys, or tap the left/right half of the game.

Lane dodgeFreedom passed: 0

The idea behind it

The red blocks are pressures, not people — the "when are you having kids?" of life, sliding down the road. You can't argue them away, but you can keep choosing your own lane. Every block you pass is one you didn't let knock you off course.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

PROJECT 060

Rationalism Toolkit

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate

A compact toolkit you can apply to any heated discussion, including this one.

Define terms

Most disputes shrink once both sides agree what words mean.

Separate facts from values

Ask: is this a disagreement about what’s true, or about what matters? They need different conversations.

Check for fallacies

Watch for appeals to nature, popularity, and authority — on every side, including your own.

Hold uncertainty

It’s fine to say 'I’m not sure.' Honest uncertainty beats false confidence.

Reflection prompt

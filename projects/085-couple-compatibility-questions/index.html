PROJECT 085

Couple Compatibility Questions

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner

Not a test — a set of prompts to discuss together. Pick what resonates and talk it through.

1. How firmly do you each feel about (not) having children?
We’ve never really discussed it
We’ve talked and broadly agree
We disagree and avoid it

2. How do you handle family pressure as a team?
Separately and inconsistently
With a shared, agreed approach
We haven’t faced it yet

3. What does a fulfilling shared future look like?
We picture it differently
We share a broad vision
We haven’t pictured it

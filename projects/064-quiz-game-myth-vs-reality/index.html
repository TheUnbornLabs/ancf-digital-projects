Quiz Game: Myth vs Reality

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · quiz game

Myth or reality? Read each statement and decide — then see the explanation. A quick, friendly way to test (and sharpen) what you know about antinatalism and childfree life. There are no trick questions, just a score, a streak, and a little learning each round.

Key takeaway

A lot of "common knowledge" about this topic is myth dressed as fact. Pausing to ask "is that actually true?" — and checking — is a small habit that pays off well beyond a quiz. However you scored, the explanations are the real prize.

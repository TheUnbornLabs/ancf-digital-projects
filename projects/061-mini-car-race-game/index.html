② The little metaphor

The road is ordinary life. The blocks are outside demands. The car is your attention. Good play is not aggression; it is timely movement. You do not have to smash every pressure. Often, the cleanest answer is to steer past it and keep possession of your direction.

This is still only a small game. Its job is not to preach. Its job is to make one thing visible: pressure keeps coming, but collision is not the only possible response.

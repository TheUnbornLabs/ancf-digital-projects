PROJECT 019

Childfree Couple Planning Board

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner · ~15 min · planning board

A childfree life is a canvas, not a blank. This board helps a couple — or anyone — name what they're building together across six areas of life. Fill what inspires you and leave the rest; the goal is a shared picture of a life chosen on purpose. Everything stays on this device.

① The idea② Your board③ Your shared vision④ Quick check⑤ Reflection

① The idea

Why a board — "we're not having kids" says what a life isn't. A board says what it is. Naming the adventures, the home, the people, and the contributions you want turns an open future into an intention you can share and return to.

Add your own items, or tap a spark to start. Nothing is uploaded.

Key takeaway

The happiest childfree lives aren't defined by absence; they're full of things chosen on purpose. A board like this is a small act of authorship — a way to say, together, "this is the life we're making." Come back to it; let it grow as you do.

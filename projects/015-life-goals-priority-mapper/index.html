PROJECT 015

Life Goals Priority Mapper

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~8 min

A clear life is easier to build than a vague one. Add the goals that matter to you and sort them across three horizons — Now, Soon, Someday — to see where your energy belongs. Everything stays on this device.

What you will learn

  • How to externalise your goals so you can actually see them.
  • How to sort by time-horizon rather than vague urgency.
  • Where your goals cluster (shown as a live chart).
  • How to export your map to keep or revisit.

1. Add a goal

Each goal starts in “Now”. Use the buttons on a goal to move it to Soon or Someday, or remove it.

Key takeaway

A life takes shape through the goals you actually name and place. Sorting them by horizon turns a vague sense of "I should…" into a small, doable "next" — and reminds you that "Someday" goals are real, just not yet.

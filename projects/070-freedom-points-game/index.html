Freedom Points Game

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · choices game

Five everyday moments, one choice each. Pick the response that protects your autonomy and stays kind. The points are just a playful mirror — and good practice for the real thing. No response is "wrong"; some simply keep more of both your freedom and your warmth.

Key takeaway

Protecting your autonomy and staying kind aren't opposites — the highest-scoring answers do both at once. Caving gives away your freedom; snapping spends your warmth; "I hear you, and this is mine" keeps both. It's a small skill, and like any skill, it grows with practice.

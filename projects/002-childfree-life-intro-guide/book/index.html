The Childfree Life

A warm, practical, honest companion to building a full and intentional life without children — from the decision itself to money, work, health, aging, relationships, and meaning.

Seven parts · 44 chapters · deciding, the practical life, relationships, social pressure, the inner life, and living it well

Start reading →Back to the interactive guide

How to use this book

  • It reads front to back, but every chapter stands alone — jump to whatever you need most right now.
  • Use the arrow keys (← →) or the buttons at the foot of each chapter to move along; a progress bar at the top tracks your place.
  • It is affirming but honest: supportive of the choice, while facing the hard parts — regret, pressure, aging, disagreement — squarely.
  • It respects people who choose children just as much. Choosing one path never requires putting down the other.
This book is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice. For decisions about health, money, or law, please consult a qualified professional.

Before you begin

Whether you are certain, undecided, or simply curious, you are welcome here. This book won't tell you what to choose — it will help you choose well, and then live your choice with confidence, planning, and joy.

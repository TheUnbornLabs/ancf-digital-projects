Article Outline Generator

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate

Scaffold a fair, well-structured article outline. Choose a topic and framing — every framing builds in room for definitions, the opposing view, and a respectful close. Then flesh it out in the editable box.

1. Build your outline

2. Flesh it out

3. Tips

Define before you argue

Sort out what "selfish", "natural", or "family" mean early, so the rest of the piece isn't arguing past itself.

Give the other side its best shot

The "opposing view, fairly stated" section is what separates persuasion from a rant. Make it strong.

Cite real sources

Use genuine, checkable references — never invented studies or quotes. Add disclaimers for anything resembling advice.

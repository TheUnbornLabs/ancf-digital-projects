PROJECT 062

Pong Game: Choice vs Pressure

Games · Difficulty: Beginner

Keep your paddle — "Choice" — in the rally against "Pressure". First to 7 wins. Light, themed fun, no people involved.

Controls: move your mouse or drag a finger over the game, or use the ↑ / ↓ arrow keys.

First to 7Choice 0 · Pressure 0

The idea behind it

"Pressure" never stops coming — it just keeps bouncing back. You don't win by destroying it; you win by staying in the rally, returning each push calmly, until your own Choice carries the day. A small metaphor for holding a boundary over time.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

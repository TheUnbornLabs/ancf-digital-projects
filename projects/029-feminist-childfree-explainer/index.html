PROJECT 029

Feminist Childfree Explainer

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~16 min · interactive

Feminist thought has long defended a woman's right to decide whether and when to have children — including the right to have none. This explainer connects those threads honestly, traces the key voices, and looks squarely at the debates within feminism. It defends no single line — feminism never spoke with one voice — but maps the terrain.

Key takeaway

The common thread isn't a verdict on motherhood — many feminists are joyful mothers — but the insistence that motherhood be a choice, freely made, equally respected whichever way it goes. A feminism worth the name defends the woman who wants six children and the one who wants none with the very same logic.

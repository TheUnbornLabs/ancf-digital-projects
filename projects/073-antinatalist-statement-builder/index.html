Antinatalist Statement Builder

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~12 min · builder

Draft a clear, careful statement of an antinatalist view — one that argues the idea and never demeans people. Pick the basis and the tone, set a length, and the builder assembles a structured statement: claim, reasoning, and an explicit note that this is about ethics, not a judgement of parents. A respect check confirms the tone.

Key takeaway

The strongest statement of a hard view is also the most careful one: it makes its case on the merits, concedes the difficulty, and explicitly refuses to demean the people who choose otherwise. That isn't softness — it's what makes the argument worth taking seriously.

PROJECT 021

Antinatalist Quote Archive

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~12 min · study archive

A study archive of original prompts that rephrase ideas from the antinatalist and pessimist traditions into plain language for reflection. These are not quotations — they're fresh wordings written to help you think with each idea. Search or filter by theme and thinker, draw one at random, save your favourites, and copy any to keep.

Key takeaway

Ideas are easier to weigh when they're stated plainly and met one at a time. However you respond to any of these prompts — agreement, resistance, or a long pause — the practice of thinking with them, rather than past them, is the whole point.

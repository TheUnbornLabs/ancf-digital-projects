Childfree FAQ

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner

Common questions, answered calmly and respectfully.

Isn’t a childfree life lonely?

No more than any life. Connection comes from relationships, community, and purpose — none of which require parenting.

Will you regret it?

Some do, many don’t; the same is true of becoming a parent. A thoughtful decision is what matters.

Who will care for you when you’re older?

Care comes from planning, friendships, chosen family, and community — children are not a guarantee of care.

Do you dislike children?

Often not at all. Many childfree people love the children in their lives; they simply don’t want to raise their own.

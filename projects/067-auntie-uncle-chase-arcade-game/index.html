“Auntie Uncle Chase” Arcade Game

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · a 2-min break

Scoot around collecting gold "freedom tokens" while keeping your distance from the well-meaning "pressure" chasers. It's all friendly metaphor — nobody's the villain, you're just keeping your space.

How to play

  • Move: arrow keys, WASD, or the on-screen pad.
  • Collect the gold token for points; avoid the red chasers.
  • It gets trickier; best is saved on this device.

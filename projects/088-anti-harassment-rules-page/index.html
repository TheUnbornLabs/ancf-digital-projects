Anti-Harassment Rules Page

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner

A ready-to-adapt rules page that keeps discussion safe and focused on ideas — protecting every member equally. Read the rules, then copy a clean version for your group.

The rules

1. Zero tolerance for harassment

No personal attacks, stalking, threats, doxxing, or targeted pile-ons. Critique ideas, never people.

2. Protected respect for all groups

No hate or slurs toward any group — parents, children, women, men, castes, religions, ethnicities, disabled people, or LGBTQ+ people. Safety is for everyone, including those who disagree with us.

3. Disagree well

Debate the claim, not the person. Steelman before you reply; concede good points; keep your tone kind.

4. No graphic or unsafe content

Nothing encouraging self-harm or violence. Support and resources, not detail. (See our crisis-safe policy.)

5. Privacy

Don't share others' personal information or screenshots without consent. What's shared here stays here.

6. Reporting & enforcement

Report concerns privately to a moderator. Mods act promptly and fairly — usually a warning, then removal for repeat or serious harm.

Reporting & response process

  1. Report privately to a named moderator or a reporting channel.
  2. Acknowledge the report quickly, even if the decision takes longer.
  3. Review fairly, hearing both sides where appropriate.
  4. Act proportionately: a private warning, content removal, or removal for serious or repeated harm.
  5. Protect the reporter from retaliation, and follow up.

Adopt these rules

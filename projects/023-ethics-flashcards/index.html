PROJECT 023

Ethics Flashcards

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~15 min · study deck

A study deck of 24 flip-cards for the key terms in birth ethics and reproductive autonomy. Tap a card to reveal its definition, mark the ones you know, and watch your progress fill. When you're ready, run a focused study session to test yourself one card at a time.

① The deck② Study session③ Quick check④ Reflection

① The deck

Tap a card (or press Enter) to flip it. Use the small badge to mark a card as known. Filter by category.

② Study session

A focused round: see a term, recall the meaning, flip to check, then mark whether you knew it. You can study the whole deck or only the cards you haven't mastered yet.

④ Your reflection

Key takeaway

A debate is far easier to follow once its vocabulary is second nature. Master these two dozen terms and the arguments across this archive — and most of moral philosophy about birth — stop feeling like jargon and start feeling like a conversation you can join.

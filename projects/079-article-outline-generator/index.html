PROJECT 079

Article Outline Generator

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~7 min

Scaffold a fair, well-structured article outline. Pick a topic and framing, and the outline includes built-in space for the opposing view — because the most persuasive writing takes objections seriously. Edit and copy.

What you will learn

  • The skeleton of a balanced article.
  • Why including the opposing view strengthens your case.
  • How a synthesis section earns trust.

1. Build an outline

2. Quick check

1. Including the opposing view tends to…
Weaken your article
Strengthen it and build trust

2. A synthesis section…
Restates without thought
Weighs the views and earns trust
Is filler

Key takeaway

A balanced outline isn't weaker — it's more persuasive. Make your case, give the opposing view its due, then synthesise. Readers trust a writer who has clearly listened.

Marriage and Children Conversation Tool

Community Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~10 min

Few conversations matter more for a couple than the one about children. This tool helps you map where you and your partner align across the key questions, see it as a chart, and prepare honest talking points — so the conversation is clearer and kinder.

A preparation aid for reflection, not relationship, legal, or mental health advice. For one person's use; share results only if you both choose to.

What you will learn

  • The core questions a couple benefits from discussing.
  • Where you currently align, differ, or are unsure (as a chart).
  • How "readiness" grows as you think each topic through.
  • How to turn it into talking points you can bring to the conversation.

Key takeaway

This is about understanding, not winning. Some differences resolve with time and care; others are real and deserve honesty rather than avoidance. Either way, naming where you stand — kindly and clearly — is the foundation of a fair decision made together.

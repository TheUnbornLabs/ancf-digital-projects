PROJECT 019

Childfree Couple Planning Board

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner · ~9 min

A childfree life is a canvas, not a blank. This board helps a couple (or anyone) name what they're building together across six areas of life. Fill what inspires you; leave the rest. Everything stays on this device.

What you will learn

  • Six areas a shared life can flourish in beyond parenthood.
  • How to turn vague hopes into written, revisitable plans.
  • Where your board is rich and where it's still blank (a completion meter).
  • How to export the board to keep or share.

Key takeaway

A shared life grows from what you choose to build, not from what you opt out of. A board you can see and revisit turns "someday" into a plan — and reminds you that a childfree life can be every bit as full, connected, and meaningful as any other.

② The little metaphor

The ball is Choice. The paddle is attention. The pressure blocks are the slogans, gossip, status expectations, timelines, and family commands that try to change the angle of your life.

A clean return is not a violent answer. It is controlled timing. You do not need to defeat every voice. You need enough steadiness to keep Choice in play.

PROJECT 054

Study Circle Dashboard

Community Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate

A private planning board for a respectful reading group. Jot this week's topic, the questions you'll discuss, who brings what, and ideas to park for later — then copy the board to share in your group chat. Nothing leaves this device unless you copy it.

How to use it

Add one item per line in each column. Tap a starter prompt to drop a question into "Discussion questions". When you're ready, "Copy our board" gives you a clean text version to paste anywhere.

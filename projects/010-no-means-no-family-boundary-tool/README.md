# "No Means No" Family Boundary Tool

*Project 010 · Category: Community Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate*

A practical toolkit for holding a calm, firm line on repeated family questions about not having
children — without burning the relationship down.

Modules: how boundaries work (the FOG — Fear/Obligation/Guilt — the broken-record technique, and
why you don't owe a justification); a **boundary script generator** (by questioner, question,
tone, and goal); an **emotional-blackmail decoder** that flags seven manipulation tactics with
steady responses; an interactive **escalation ladder** (six rungs from light redirect to
follow-through); a **hold-the-line rehearsal**; a quiz; and reflection.

Vanilla JS on the shared `../../ancf-ui.js` toolkit; verified with a headless DOM test (11/11).
Nothing is uploaded.

> This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or
> mental health advice. If pressure involves threats or fear for your safety, consider reaching
> out to a trusted person or professional.

“Is This My Choice?” Reflection Tool

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner · ~9 min

This tool helps you tell the difference between what you want and what you have been told to want. Everything stays on this device. There are no correct answers — only a clearer view of your own decision.

What you will learn

  • How your desire and the pressure you feel compare, side by side.
  • An autonomy reading: how much this choice feels like yours right now.
  • How to separate your internal voice from the external voices around you.
  • Which signs of decision-clarity are already present for you.

Tick what feels true right now. This is a clarity reading, not a pass/fail. Saved on this device.

4. Scenario

Imagine: you make this decision and tell no one at all. No approval, no disapproval — just you and the choice.

Notice your body's first reaction. Relief usually points toward your own voice; dread often points toward an external one. Sit with whichever shows up — there is no wrong answer.

5. Your clarity report

Saved only on this device.

The report gathers your desire/pressure, autonomy reading, clarity signs, and notes. Saved on this device only.

Key takeaway

Autonomy is not a particular answer — it is the freedom to reach your own. Whatever you concluded, you took time to ask what you actually want, and that is the whole practice. You can return whenever the question feels alive again.

If these reflections stir up distress that feels hard to carry alone, please consider reaching out to someone you trust or a qualified professional. You deserve support.

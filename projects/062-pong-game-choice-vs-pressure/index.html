Pong: Choice vs Pressure

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~5 min · arcade

Keep "Choice" bouncing and don't let it drop. Each time your paddle sends it back, you score — and it speeds up a little. A light reminder that staying in the game just takes a steady paddle and a calm eye. Move with the arrow keys, the buttons, or by dragging across the court.

Key takeaway

Pressure, like the ball, tends to come back faster the longer things go on. You don't have to smash it away — just keep your paddle under it, calm and ready. Staying in the game is its own kind of winning. Play for fun; hold the metaphor lightly.

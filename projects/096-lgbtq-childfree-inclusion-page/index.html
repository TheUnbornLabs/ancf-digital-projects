LGBTQ+ Childfree Inclusion

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~15 min · inclusion page

LGBTQ+ people belong fully in conversations about childfree life and reproductive autonomy. Paths to family are wonderfully varied — and so is the equally valid choice to remain childfree. This page names the assumptions that get in the way, affirms every freely-made choice, and simply says: you're welcome here.

Key takeaway

For LGBTQ+ people, the assumptions cut both ways — sometimes "you can't" or "you won't," sometimes pressure to prove family by having children. The answer to all of it is the same: every path to family, and the equally valid path of remaining childfree, deserves full respect. You define your family. Always.

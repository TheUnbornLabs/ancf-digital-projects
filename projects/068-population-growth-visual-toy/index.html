PROJECT 068

Population Growth Visual Toy

Games · Difficulty: Beginner

Slide the growth rate to watch an exponential curve (red) pull away from steady linear growth (grey). It's a neutral maths visual about how compounding works — not a claim about any group of people.

Drag the slider below to change the growth rate.

Exponential vs. linear

Red = exponential (compounding). Grey = linear (same amount added each step). The "rule of 70" estimates doubling time ≈ 70 ÷ rate.

A important honesty note

This toy shows the maths of compounding, which applies to savings, bacteria, or anything that grows by a percentage. Real human population is not a simple exponential: global growth rates have been falling for decades, and demography depends on health, education, and choice — not a single slider. Treat this as a lesson in curves, not a statement about people.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

PROJECT 063

Platform Game: Boundary Runner

Games · Difficulty: Beginner

Hop over the red blocks to "keep your boundaries" and see how far you get. A light endless runner — the blocks are pressures, not people.

Controls: the big Jump button below, the Space bar, or tap the game.

Endless runnerBoundaries kept: 0

The idea behind it

Each block is a moment of pressure rolling toward you. You don't fight it — you clear it, and keep running your own way. Every boundary kept is a small win, and the game speeds up just like real life does around the holidays.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

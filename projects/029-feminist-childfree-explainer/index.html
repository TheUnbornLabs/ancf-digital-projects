PROJECT 029

Feminist Childfree Explainer

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate

A respectful explainer linking autonomy, equality, and the freedom to choose parenthood or not. It honours women who parent and women who don’t.

Autonomy at the core

Feminist ethics emphasises that women — and all people — should control their own reproductive lives.

Beyond a single script

Worth is not tied to motherhood. Choosing to parent and choosing not to are both fully valid.

Solidarity, not division

Supporting the childfree choice does not diminish mothers; it widens respect for everyone’s decisions.

Reflection prompt

How has the 'expected path' shaped choices in your own life or community?

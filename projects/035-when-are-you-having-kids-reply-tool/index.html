“When Are You Having Kids?” Reply Tool

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner

The most common question of all deserves a ready answer. Pick a vibe, cycle through polite one-liners until one feels like you, and copy it. Every option stays kind — you can hold a boundary without a sharp edge.

Get a one-liner

Your one-liner will appear here.

How to deliver it

Say it lightly, then move on

The tone matters more than the words. A relaxed delivery and a quick subject-change signals "this isn't a debate" without any sting.

Repetition beats escalation

If they push, just repeat your line once more, warmly. You don't need a new answer — the same calm one, again, does the work.

You can stop at "no"

"We're not planning to" is a complete answer. You're never obliged to explain your reasons.

Time Freedom Calculator

Calculators · Difficulty: Beginner

A transparent time model. Adjust to your reality; it simply does arithmetic with the hours you enter.

Enter values and press Calculate.

Formula / logic:

free_per_day = 24 - sleep - work - care; free_per_week = free_per_day * 7

Couple Compatibility Questions

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner · ~20 min · question deck

Whether to have children is one of the biggest things partners can align on — and it's far better discussed early and openly than assumed. Work through this deck together, mark where you're aligned or need to talk, and see where to focus. There are no right answers, only honest ones.

Key takeaway

The kindest thing two people can do about the big questions is ask them out loud, early, without assuming. "Need to talk" isn't a red flag — it's exactly what this is for. A relationship strong enough to name its differences honestly is a relationship with real foundations.

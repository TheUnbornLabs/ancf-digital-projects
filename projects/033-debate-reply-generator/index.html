PROJECT 033

Debate Reply Generator

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~7 min

Draft a calm, non-hostile reply to a common claim about not having children. Pick the claim and a style, and get a reply aimed squarely at the idea — never at the person. Edit it to fit your voice.

For respectful discussion. These replies target arguments, not people. Disagreement is not disrespect — and you never owe anyone a debate.

What you will learn

  • How to answer a claim without attacking the speaker.
  • How calm, curious, and firm styles differ.
  • How to keep a reply short and steady.
  • When it's wiser to decline the debate entirely.

Key takeaway

Win the point, keep the person. Aim at the idea, stay calm, and remember the most powerful move is sometimes to decline the debate and change the subject.

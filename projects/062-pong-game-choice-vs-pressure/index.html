PROJECT 062

Pong: Choice vs Pressure

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · a 2-min break

Keep "Choice" bouncing and don't let it drop. The longer you rally, the faster it gets — a light reminder that staying in the game just takes a steady hand.

How to play

  • Move the paddle: Left/Right arrows, A/D, or the on-screen buttons.
  • Goal: keep the ball from falling past your paddle.
  • Each bounce scores a point; best is saved on this device.

Play

Rally: 0Best: 0

Press Start (or an arrow key) to play.

Your reflection

Saved on this device only. Nothing is uploaded.

Key takeaway

You don't have to be perfect — just keep the ball in play. Small, steady moves keep your choice alive, even as the pace picks up.

PROJECT 008

Suffering Risk Thought Experiment

Philosophy · Difficulty: Intermediate

This is a reflection exercise, not a test. Sit with each prompt and notice your reasoning, not just your conclusion.

Imagine you could guarantee a life of mostly joy but with some unavoidable pain. Would the guarantee change your view? Why?
Now remove the guarantee — outcomes are uncertain. Does uncertainty itself carry moral weight?
Does the possibility of great meaning offset the certainty of some suffering, in your view?
What would you want someone to consider before making this decision for a future person?
This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

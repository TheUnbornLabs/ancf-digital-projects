# Article Outline Generator

*Project 079 · Category: Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate*

Generates a structured article outline from a topic, framing (persuasive/balanced/explainer/personal),
and body-section count — every framing reserves a built-in opposing-view / objections / counter-case
section. Live, copyable, with cards on why fairness strengthens persuasion. Vanilla JS; DOM test 8/8.

PROJECT 060

Rationalism Toolkit

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~20 min · toolkit

Seven practical tools for thinking clearly about emotive topics — including this one. Learn each tool, run any of them on a claim you choose, and audit how often you reach for them. Use them on others' arguments and, harder still, on your own. The aim isn't to win, but to be a little less wrong.

⑤ Your reflection

Saved on this device only.

Key takeaway

Clear thinking is a set of habits, not a talent — and the real test is whether you'll turn them on your own cherished views, not just your opponents'. None of these tools tells you what to conclude about having children. They just help you reach whatever you conclude more honestly.

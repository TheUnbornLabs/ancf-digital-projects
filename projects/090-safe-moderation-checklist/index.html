Safe Moderation Checklist

Community Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~8 min

Moderating a community well is quiet, steady work. This checklist gathers the habits that keep a space safe and fair — and includes care for the moderators themselves, because looking after the room shouldn't cost you your own peace.

What you will learn

  • Habits that keep moderation calm and consistent.
  • How fairness and transparency build trust.
  • Why moderator self-care is part of the job.

Key takeaway

Good moderation is consistency plus care — for the community and for the moderators. Apply rules fairly, explain kindly, signpost support, and protect your own team's wellbeing. A healthy mod team makes a healthy room.

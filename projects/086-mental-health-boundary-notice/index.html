Mental Health Boundary Notice

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · generator

Online spaces about big life topics can get heavy. This helps you draft a short, caring notice — for a group, post, or thread — that sets a gentle boundary, asks for content warnings, or points people toward support. Warm and non-clinical by design; it cares for the space without diagnosing anyone.

Key takeaway

A few caring words, posted at the right moment, can change the temperature of a whole space. You don't need clinical language or grand gestures — just warmth, a clear ask, and a reminder that stepping back is always okay. Tending a community's wellbeing is quiet, ordinary kindness.

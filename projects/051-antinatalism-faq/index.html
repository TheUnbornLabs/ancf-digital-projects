Antinatalism FAQ

Philosophy · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~20 min · reference

Clear, respectful answers to frequent questions and misconceptions about antinatalism — the philosophical view that assigns a negative value to coming into existence. This isn't advocacy: it explains the position fairly, flags common myths, and notes where thoughtful people disagree. Filter by topic, search, open a question, and copy any answer.

Key takeaway

Antinatalism is a serious philosophical position, not a mood or a wish for harm — and like any position, it has both careful arguments and reasonable objections. Understanding it clearly, myths and all, lets you engage it on its merits, whether you end up agreeing, disagreeing, or somewhere in between.

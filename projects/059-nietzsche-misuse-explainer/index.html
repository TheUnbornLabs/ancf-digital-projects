Nietzsche Misuse Explainer

Philosophy · Difficulty: Advanced

A corrective explainer: Nietzsche is frequently flattened into slogans. This restores some nuance.

Common misuse

Lines about strength or 'amor fati' get cropped into crude takes on having or not having children.

A fairer reading

Nietzsche prized affirming life and creating values; he resists tidy use by any 'side' of this debate.

The lesson

Quote carefully and in context — borrowed authority is not an argument.

Reflection prompt

Have you seen a thinker quoted out of context? What was lost?

WhatsApp Group Rules Generator

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~6 min

Clear rules make a kinder group. Pick your group's focus and how strict you want moderation to be, and generate a ready-to-pin rules set — inclusive and safety-first by design. Edit it to fit, then copy.

What you will learn

  • The core rules every healthy group needs.
  • How focus (support, study, activism) changes the rules.
  • How strictness shapes moderation.
  • How to keep rules welcoming, not just restrictive.

Key takeaway

The best rules say what the group is for, not just what's banned. Lead with welcome and care, name the bright lines clearly, and members will help you hold them.

Sterilization Discussion Preparation Tool

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~15 min · prep tool

If you're considering a conversation with a provider about permanent contraception, this builds a tidy list of questions to ask — tailored to the topics you choose. It helps you walk in prepared and be heard. This is educational preparation, not medical advice; only a qualified provider can give clinical guidance for your situation.

Please note: Nothing here recommends or discourages any procedure, or describes how one is done. It only helps you organise questions to ask a professional. Decisions and clinical details belong with your provider.

Key takeaway

A clear list of questions turns a big, personal conversation into a manageable one. You deserve full, respectful information and to have your informed choice taken seriously. Bring your questions, take your time, and remember you can always ask for more information or another opinion.

## The Antinatalism Reader (full book)

This project now includes a full-length educational companion book at `book/index.html`:
43 chapters across seven parts (Foundations, Core Arguments, Thinkers, Varieties & Neighbors,
Objections & Replies, In Context, and Living With the Question) plus glossary, timeline,
further reading, and FAQ. Roughly 58,000 words. Every major argument is paired with its
strongest objection. Built on the shared ANCF design system; `book/book.css` and `book/book.js`
add the reading layout, progress bar, and chapter navigation.

Debate Notes Organizer

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~15 min · organizer

Prepare for a respectful discussion by sorting your thoughts into four boxes: your claims, their strongest points, the evidence, and the common ground. Filling the "their strongest points" box is steelmanning in action — and the surest sign you're ready to disagree well. Export a tidy prep sheet when you're done.

Key takeaway

The best debate prep isn't a list of zingers — it's an honest map of the whole disagreement, the other side's strongest case included. When you can state their view fairly and name where you actually agree, you're ready not to win, but to understand and be understood.

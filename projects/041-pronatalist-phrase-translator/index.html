Pronatalist Phrase Translator

Society · Difficulty: Beginner

Common pressure phrases often carry a hidden assumption. This tool "translates" a phrase into what it usually assumes underneath — and offers a kind reply. The goal is understanding, not blame: most of these are said with love.

3. Why "translate" instead of argue?

Naming the assumption underneath a phrase does two useful things: it stops you taking the comment personally, and it lets you respond to the real concern (often love or fear) rather than the clumsy words. That's why every reply here starts by acknowledging the care before holding the boundary.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

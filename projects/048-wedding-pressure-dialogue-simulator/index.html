Wedding Pressure Dialogue Simulator

Society · Difficulty: Beginner · ~8 min

Weddings are peak season for "you're next!" This short, branching simulator lets you rehearse calm responses in a safe space and see how each choice tends to land — so the real moment feels easier. There are no wrong answers, only practice.

What you will learn

  • How different responses tend to land in the moment.
  • How to stay warm while holding a boundary.
  • How to redirect a conversation gracefully.

Pronatalist Phrase Translator

Society · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · translator

Common pressure phrases often carry a hidden assumption. This translator shows the assumption underneath a phrase — what it literally says, what it really means, and a kind, clear reply. Type or paste a phrase to find the closest match, or browse the library. It's for understanding the pressure, never for winning a fight.

Key takeaway

A pressure phrase loses much of its power the moment you can see what it quietly assumes. Most are said with love, not malice — translating them isn't about catching anyone out, it's about hearing the worry underneath and answering that, calmly, on your own terms.

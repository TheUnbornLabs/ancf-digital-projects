Key takeaway

Good moderation is mostly invisible: clear rules applied consistently, conflict handled early and calmly, and decisions made with care. But a community is only as healthy as the people holding it — so the most overlooked item on any moderation checklist is the moderator's own wellbeing. Tend the space, and tend yourself.

Key takeaway

Most obstacles aren't walls — they're hurdles. You don't stop, you just lift your feet for a beat and carry on. The rhythm of "see it, clear it, keep going" is a surprisingly good way to handle the small pressures, too. Play for fun; take the metaphor lightly.

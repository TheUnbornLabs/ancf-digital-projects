PROJECT 092

Tamil Childfree Voices Page

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate

This page honours Tamil readers and the rationalist self-respect tradition — presented respectfully, accurately, and without speaking for anyone. It connects shared values like dignity and autonomy; it does not claim any community endorses a particular choice.

Self-respect and choice

A tradition of dignity

Tamil rationalist thought, associated with the Self-Respect Movement (Suya Mariyadai Iyakkam) and figures like Periyar, has long valued human dignity, reason, and freedom from imposed social roles — including a notable emphasis on women's autonomy. These values resonate naturally with reproductive autonomy. (See also the Periyarism reflection in this collection.)

Family and warmth

Strong family bonds and personal choice are not opposites. Many people stay deeply connected to family and culture while making their own decisions about children. Honouring both is the aim.

Language and belonging

Reflecting in your first language can make a feeling clearer and more your own. You're warmly invited to think — and, if you wish, write — in Tamil or whatever language is closest to your heart.

An invitation

This page doesn't put words in anyone's mouth. It simply offers a respectful space and a few shared values — dignity, reason, autonomy — as a starting point. The choice, and how you frame it, remain entirely yours.

Comic Strip Prompt Generator

Comics · Difficulty: Beginner

Build a wholesome comic premise that uses metaphor and humour about pressure — never abuse toward people. Pick a character, theme, and format, then generate a panel-by-panel sketch you can draw or adapt.

3. Tips for a kind comic

Punch at the pressure, not the people

The "villain" is an expectation, a cliché, or a swirl of guilt — never a real group. Parents, relatives, and anyone who disagrees keep their dignity.

Let the character win gently

The best ending isn't a comeback that humiliates someone — it's the character calmly choosing themselves, with warmth left intact.

Metaphor does the heavy lifting

An over-full teapot, a plant reaching for light, a robot learning "no" — gentle metaphors say more than a lecture, and travel further.

PROJECT 024

Philosophy Timeline

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~9 min

A simplified study timeline of ideas that feed into antinatalist and childfree thought — across philosophy, religion, feminism, and ecology. Filter by strand and tap an entry to expand it. This is a broad overview for orientation, not a complete history.

An educational overview. Dates are approximate and entries are simplified; consult primary sources for depth. Not an endorsement of any single view.

What you will learn

  • How reflection on suffering and existence appears across many traditions.
  • A few landmark works that shaped modern debate.
  • How different strands (philosophy, religion, feminism, ecology) interweave.
  • Where to look next for deeper study.

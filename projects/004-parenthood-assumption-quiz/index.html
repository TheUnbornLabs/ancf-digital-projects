Parenthood Assumption Quiz

Society · Difficulty: Beginner · ~9 min

Everyday talk about parenthood is full of quiet assumptions. This quiz helps you name the assumption behind a phrase, then map which assumptions have shaped you and where each one came from. There are no wrong people here — only ideas to weigh.

What you will learn

  • Seven common assumption types: tradition, fear, status, duty, loneliness, gender role, economics.
  • How to spot which assumption is hiding inside a common phrase.
  • Which of these assumptions have shaped your own thinking.
  • Where a belief came from — so you can decide whether to keep it.

Key takeaway

Noticing an assumption is not about catching anyone out — it is about giving everyone, including yourself, a little more room. A belief you can trace and examine is one you actually own, whatever you decide to do with it.

Mods are people too

Moderating exposes you to conflict and sometimes distressing content. Rotate duties, set hours you won't moderate, and lean on your team. A burned-out moderator can't keep anyone safe — including themselves.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

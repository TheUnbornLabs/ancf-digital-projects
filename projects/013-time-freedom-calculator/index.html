PROJECT 013

Time Freedom Calculator

Calculators · Difficulty: Beginner · ~7 min

Every week holds 168 hours. This tool shows how yours divide and how much discretionary time remains under different commitments — including caregiving. Drag the sliders to your own reality; nothing here says how anyone should spend their time.

An illustration for reflection only. Time use is personal and changes constantly — these are rough figures, not a judgement.

What you will learn

  • How the fixed 168 hours of a week get allocated.
  • How much discretionary time is left after commitments.
  • How adding caregiving hours changes the picture.
  • How weekly hours scale across a whole year.

Key takeaway

Time, like money, is one real factor among many — and caregiving time is often given gladly. The aim is a clear, honest picture of your week, not a claim that more free hours always means a better life.

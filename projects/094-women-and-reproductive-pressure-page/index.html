PROJECT 094

Women and Reproductive Pressure Page

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate

A supportive page on pressures women face, honouring those who parent and those who don’t equally.

The 'expected path'

Women often face intense expectations to marry and have children on a timeline.

Body and time pressure

Talk of 'body clocks' can add stress; information helps replace anxiety with agency.

Respect in every direction

Supporting childfree women never diminishes mothers — it widens respect for all women’s choices.

Reflection prompt

What support would make this choice feel freer for women you know?

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

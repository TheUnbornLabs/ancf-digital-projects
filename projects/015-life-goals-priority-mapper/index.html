PROJECT 015

Life-Goals Priority Mapper

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~15 min · planning tool

A clear life is easier to build than a vague one. Add the goals that matter to you, then sort them across three horizons — Now, Soon, Someday — and an importance-vs-effort map, to see where your energy actually belongs. It even suggests where to start. Everything stays on your device.

① How it works② Add your goals③ Your map④ Where to start⑤ Quick check⑥ Reflection

① How it works

The idea — most people carry a tangle of half-formed goals. Naming them, placing them in time, and weighing importance against effort turns the tangle into a map you can act on. There are no wrong goals here; the tool only helps you see them clearly.

Add goals below, set each one's horizon, importance, and effort, and the map and focus suggestion update live. Nothing is uploaded.

Key takeaway

Energy follows attention, and attention follows clarity. A childfree life — or any life — is a canvas, and the people who fill it on purpose tend to start by naming what matters and weighing it honestly. The map won't live your life for you, but it makes the next right step a lot easier to see.

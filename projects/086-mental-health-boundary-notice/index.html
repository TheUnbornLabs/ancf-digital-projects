PROJECT 086

Mental Health Boundary Notice

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner

Generate a gentle notice for a group that protects everyone's wellbeing and points to real support — making clear the space is supportive, but not a crisis service. Pick a context, then add your local resources.

1. Generate a notice

Your notice will appear here.

2. Add your real resources

3. Keep it safe

Respond to distress with care, not discipline

If someone expresses they're struggling, lead with warmth and resources — never a warning or removal for the distress itself.

Don't promise what you can't give

A community isn't a crisis service. Being honest about that — while still being kind — protects everyone, including moderators.

Have resources ready in advance

List a few real, region-appropriate support lines before you ever need them, so no one is scrambling in a hard moment.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice. If you or someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

PROJECT 040

Bad Argument Detector

Learning Tools · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~8 min

Paste any argument and spot the classic logical fallacies hiding inside — each with a plain explanation. It's a study aid for learning to reason well, not a verdict on anyone. A flagged pattern is a prompt to look closer, not proof of bad faith.

What you will learn

  • Eight common fallacies and how to recognise them.
  • Why a fallacy weakens an argument (even a popular one).
  • How to reply by pointing at the reasoning, not the person.

Key takeaway

Spotting a fallacy doesn't win an argument or condemn a person — it just shows where the reasoning is shaky. Note it kindly, ask for the stronger version, and keep the conversation honest.

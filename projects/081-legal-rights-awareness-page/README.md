# Legal Rights Awareness Page

*Project 081 · Category: Society · Difficulty: Intermediate*

A plain-language reference of six rights/documents areas (reproductive autonomy, advance directive,
power of attorney, will, beneficiary designations, emergency preferences) as expandable cards, a
saved 7-item "documents to consider" checklist with progress, and four next-step cards. Strong
non-legal-advice / varies-by-jurisdiction framing. Vanilla JS; DOM test 6/6.

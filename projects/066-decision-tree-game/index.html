PROJECT 066

Decision Tree Game

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~5 min

Walk a short, branching scenario about handling family pressure. Your choices change the path and earn "freedom points" for calm, self-respecting moves. There's no losing — just practice for the real thing.

How to play

  • Read each moment and pick how you'd respond.
  • Your choice leads somewhere new and may earn freedom points.
  • Reach an ending, then start over to try a different path.

Play

Freedom points: 0

Your reflection

Key takeaway

Every pressured moment is a branch with choices. Rehearsing the calm, self-respecting ones here makes them easier to reach when it counts.

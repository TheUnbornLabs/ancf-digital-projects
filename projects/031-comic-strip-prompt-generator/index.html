PROJECT 031

Comic Strip Prompt Generator

Comics · Difficulty: Beginner · ~10 min · generator

Comics make ideas friendly. This tool generates gentle, metaphor-based three-panel premises about pressure, autonomy, and boundaries — laid out as a storyboard you can sketch from. Pick a theme and tone, generate, render the panels, save your favourites, and copy. The metaphors keep it warm and never mean-spirited.

Key takeaway

A heavy idea, drawn lightly, gets through where a lecture can't. Metaphor lets you explore pressure and autonomy with a wink — inviting reflection instead of defensiveness. Take any premise here as a seed and make it your own.

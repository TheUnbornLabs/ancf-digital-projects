# "Is This My Choice?" Reflection Tool

*Project 005 · Category: Childfree Life · Difficulty: Intermediate*

A decision-clarity studio for one hard question: is this decision actually mine? It helps you
tell authentic desire apart from inherited pressure. Works for the children question — and for
any major life choice. The introspective companion to Projects 003 (others' pressure) and 004
(inherited assumptions); this one focuses on your own decision process.

Eight modules:
1. **What makes a choice yours** — autonomy, authentic vs introjected "shoulds," clarity vs
   certainty, reversible vs irreversible, internal vs external locus.
2. **Desire vs pressure map** — a 2x2 quadrant plotting inner desire against outside pressure,
   with an autonomy reading and tailored verdict.
3. **What matters to you** — a values clarifier: choose core values, then mark whether the
   choice honors, is neutral to, or costs each, with a running summary.
4. **Whose voice is it?** — a voice sorter: add the things you think, tag each "mine" or
   "inherited," and watch the balance.
5. **Clarity signals** — a seven-item self-reading.
6. **Perspective tools** — five time-tested techniques (the "tell no one" test, future self /
   regret-minimization, advice to a friend, 10-10-10, the coin flip), each with note capture.
7. **Quick check** — a five-question quiz.
8. **Your clarity report** — a live private summary you can copy; clear-all wipes the device.

All vanilla JS on the shared `../../ancf-ui.js` toolkit; verified with a headless DOM test
(16/16). Nothing is uploaded; everything is stored only in the browser.

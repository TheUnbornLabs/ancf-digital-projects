You deserve to be heard

You're entitled to clear information, to have your preferences taken seriously, and to ask questions until you understand. If a conversation doesn't go well, seeking another provider is a reasonable step. Being prepared makes it far more likely you'll leave informed and respected.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice. Decisions about your care belong with you and a qualified clinician.

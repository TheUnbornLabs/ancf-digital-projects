PROJECT 031

Comic Strip Prompt Generator

Comics · Difficulty: Beginner · ~6 min

Comics make ideas friendly. This tool generates gentle, metaphor-based comic premises about pressure, autonomy, and boundaries — laid out as a three-panel storyboard you can copy. It pokes fun at situations, never at real people or groups.

For kind, educational comics. Never mock parents, children, women, men, families, religious, caste, ethnic, poor, or LGBTQ+ people. Aim at ideas and situations, not at anyone's identity.

What you will learn

  • How to turn a theme into a three-beat comic: setup, turn, resolution.
  • How metaphor keeps satire kind and on-target.
  • How tone changes the same idea.
  • How to storyboard a premise before drawing.

Key takeaway

The kindest comics let everyone laugh, including the people they're about. Aim your wit at the pressure or the assumption — never at a person's worth — and the joke stays a doorway rather than a wall.

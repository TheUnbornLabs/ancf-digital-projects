PROJECT 061

Mini Car Race Game

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · a 2-min break

Steer your car between three lanes and slip past the falling "pressure" blocks. It's a light, playful metaphor — a small reminder that with a little focus, you can keep moving and let the pressure pass.

How to play

  • Move: Left/Right arrow keys, the A and D keys, or the on-screen buttons.
  • Goal: dodge the blocks as long as you can; your score climbs with distance.
  • It speeds up — your best score is saved on this device.

Play

Score: 0Best: 0

Press Start (or any arrow key) to play.

Key takeaway

Pressure keeps coming, but you get to steer. A small game, a small reminder: focus on your lane, keep moving, and most of it passes by.

PROJECT 070

Freedom Points Game

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~4 min

Five everyday moments, one choice each. Pick the response that protects your autonomy and stays kind. The points are just a playful mirror — and good practice for real life.

How to play

  • Read each moment and choose a response.
  • Self-respecting, kind choices earn the most freedom points.
  • Finish all five; your best is saved on this device.

Play

Press Start to begin.

Your reflection

Saved on this device only. Nothing is uploaded.

Key takeaway

Autonomy is a muscle. Choosing the calm, self-respecting, kind option here — again and again — makes it the natural choice when it counts.

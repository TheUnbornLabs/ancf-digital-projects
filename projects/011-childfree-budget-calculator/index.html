PROJECT 011

Childfree Budget Calculator

Calculators · Difficulty: Beginner · ~15 min · editable model

If you're planning a childfree life, some funds that often go toward raising a child may be available for other priorities. This is a rough, fully editable model — every figure is a placeholder you should replace with your own. It then helps you reallocate what's freed and project how a "freedom fund" might grow. Money is only one dimension of any life decision, and these numbers prove nothing about anyone's choice; they're a planning aid, not a scoreboard.

① How to use it

What this is — a sandbox for your own numbers. The presets are crude regional starting points; real costs vary enormously by country, city, lifestyle, and family. Replace every figure with yours, then use the reallocation and growth tools to picture what a childfree budget could build.

Pick a currency and a starting preset, then edit freely. Everything is saved on this device only — nothing is uploaded.

Key takeaway

Money freed by one choice only matters if you direct it on purpose — otherwise it quietly leaks into everyday life. Whatever you decide about children, knowing what a budget could build, and choosing where it goes, is a kind of freedom in itself. And remember: these numbers describe a budget, never a life's worth.

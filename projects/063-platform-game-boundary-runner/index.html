Platform Game: Boundary Runner

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · a 2-min break

Keep running and hop over each obstacle. A tiny endless runner — and a light metaphor for clearing the little hurdles that come at you, one jump at a time.

How to play

  • Jump: the Jump button, Space, the Up arrow, or tap the game.
  • Goal: clear the blocks; your distance is your score.
  • It speeds up; best is saved on this device.

Play

Distance: 0Best: 0

Press Start, then Jump (or Space) to hop.

Your reflection

Saved on this device only. Nothing is uploaded.

Key takeaway

You don't clear every hurdle at once — just the next one. One steady jump at a time keeps you moving forward.

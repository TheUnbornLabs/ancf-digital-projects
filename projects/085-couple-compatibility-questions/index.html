PROJECT 085

Couple Compatibility Questions

Childfree Life · Difficulty: Beginner

Not a test, and there are no "right" answers. These are prompts to talk through together. Pick the option closest to where you are, and you'll get a gentle note on what to discuss next. Honesty here is a gift to the relationship.

1. How firmly do you each feel about (not) having children?
We've never really discussed it
We've talked and broadly agree
We disagree and tend to avoid it

2. How do you handle family pressure as a team?
Separately and inconsistently
With a shared, agreed approach
We haven't faced it yet

3. What does a fulfilling shared future look like?
We picture it differently
We share a broad vision
We haven't really pictured it

4. If you disagreed deeply on this, how would you handle it?
We're not sure / haven't thought about it
We'd talk honestly, even if it's hard
We tend to bury big disagreements

Talk it through

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice. For a relationship that feels stuck, a qualified couples counsellor is a healthy resource.

Crisis-Safe Community Policy Page

Community Tools · Difficulty: Advanced

A template policy to keep communities safe. Adapt with local resources; it is guidance, not professional or legal advice.

Our stance

We never encourage self-harm or harm to others. We respond to distress with care and signposting.

What moderators do

Stay calm, respond privately and kindly, share general support resources, and avoid amplifying graphic content.

What we don’t do

We don’t diagnose, promise confidentiality we can’t guarantee, or replace professional help.

Reflection prompt

Which local or national support resources could your community list in advance?

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

WhatsApp Group Rules Generator

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner

Good rules make a group feel safe and welcoming. Pick your focus and moderation level to draft clear, inclusive, safety-first rules — then edit them to fit your community. Works for WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, or any group.

1. Generate your rules

Your rules will appear here.

2. Make them yours

3. Moderation tips

Pin the rules and the welcome

New members should see the rules and a warm welcome before they post. A pinned message does both.

Protect every group

Strong rules explicitly bar hate toward parents, children, women, men, castes, religions, ethnic groups, disabled people, and LGBTQ+ people. Safety is for everyone, including those who disagree.

Have a crisis line ready

Decide in advance how the team responds if someone expresses thoughts of self-harm: respond with care, share support resources, and never shame. (See the crisis-safe policy project later in this collection.)

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

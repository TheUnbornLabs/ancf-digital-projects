PROJECT 034

Family Function Survival Script

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~12 min · planner

Big gatherings go better with a plan. Assemble a small pocket script — a calm opener, a deflection, a firm line, and a graceful exit — tuned to the event and your style. Then preview the field guide of lines you'll likely hear (each with a ready response), and run a quick readiness checklist before you go.

Key takeaway

You can't control what anyone asks, but you can decide in advance how you'll answer. A few rehearsed lines turn a dreaded moment into a manageable one — calm, kind, and unmistakably yours. Pocket the script, and let the rest of the day be about the people you actually enjoy.

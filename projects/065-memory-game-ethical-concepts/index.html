PROJECT 065

Memory Game: Ethical Concepts

Games · Difficulty: Beginner · ~4 min

Flip the cards and match the pairs of key ethics terms. Each match reveals a quick definition — a playful way to make the vocabulary stick. Fewer moves is better!

How to play

  • Tap (or focus and press Enter) a card to flip it.
  • Flip two; if they match, they stay up and show a definition.
  • Match all six pairs in as few moves as possible; best is saved.

PROJECT 091

Global Childfree Voices Map

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate

A reflective overview of broad conversational themes about childfree life in different parts of the world. These are themes drawn from public discussion — not claims about any place or person. People everywhere hold every view, and the diversity within any region is far greater than the difference between regions.

Explore by region

Tap a region to read a note. No external services or tracking are used.

Choose a region above.

A note on maps like this

Any "map" of human attitudes risks flattening real people into a single line. This one tries hard not to: every region contains people who want children, people who don't, and people still deciding — across every culture, faith, and class. Treat the notes as starting points for curiosity, never as labels for anyone you meet.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

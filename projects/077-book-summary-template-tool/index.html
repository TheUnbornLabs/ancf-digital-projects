Book Summary Template Tool

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~8 min

A simple, reusable template for summarising any book you read — capturing the thesis, key ideas, a few lines, your own critique, and one takeaway. Fill it in, watch the meter, and copy your summary. Great for a reading journal.

What you will learn

  • The parts of a useful book summary.
  • Why your own critique matters, not just the author's claims.
  • How a single takeaway makes a book stick.

Key takeaway

Summarise to understand, not just to remember. Capturing the thesis and your own response turns reading into thinking — and one clear takeaway makes it last.

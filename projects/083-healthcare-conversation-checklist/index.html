PROJECT 083

Healthcare Conversation Checklist

Practical Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~7 min

A simple way to prepare for a clear, respectful conversation with a healthcare provider — what to think through before, what to cover during, and what to confirm after. Walking in prepared helps you be heard.

This is a preparation aid, not medical advice. It doesn't recommend any procedure or decision. Your provider is the right person for questions about your health.

What you will learn

  • How to organise your thoughts before an appointment.
  • Ways to ask questions and confirm you understood.
  • What to note down afterwards.

Key takeaway

Preparation turns a rushed appointment into a real conversation. Bring your questions, check your understanding, note the next steps — and remember your provider, not any checklist, is the one to advise on your health.

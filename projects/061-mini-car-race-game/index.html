Key takeaway

It's only a little game — but there's a grain of truth in it. Pressure tends to keep coming; you don't have to crash into it. A small adjustment here, a clear look ahead there, and you keep moving in the direction you chose. Play for fun, and take the metaphor as lightly as you like.

PROJECT 069

Resource Allocation Simulator

Games · Difficulty: Beginner

A live, neutral illustration of simple division: how a fixed pool splits per person as a household grows. It updates as you drag. This is arithmetic, not a statement about anyone's worth, choices, or family size.

Split a pool

The honest caveat

If a pool is truly fixed, more people means a smaller slice each — that's just arithmetic. But real life is not a fixed pool: people also produce resources, share, and innovate, and households are not zero-sum. So please don't read this as "more people = worse." It's a tool for thinking about division, nothing more.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

Cultural Pressure Map

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate

Pressure to have children comes from many "regions" at once. This is a reflective map of common sources — and an interactive one: rate how strongly each presses on you to see your own map. It's about patterns, never a claim about any specific person or culture. Everything stays on this device.

What to do with your map

Seeing where pressure concentrates is the first step to handling it. The loudest region is usually the one to prepare for — a ready reply for elders, a muted feed if it's media, a chosen community if it's belonging. And if your strongest source is the inner voice, that's worth noticing too: some of what feels like your own conviction may be borrowed, and you're allowed to question it gently.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

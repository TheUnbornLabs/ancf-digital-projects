Retirement Without Children Planner

Calculators · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~9 min

"Who will look after you when you're old?" is better answered by planning than by having children. This simple projector estimates how savings might grow toward later-life security — through your own savings, community, and services rather than relying on anyone.

An illustration for reflection, not financial advice. Real returns vary and are never guaranteed; inflation and taxes are not modelled. For decisions, consult a qualified, independent adviser.

What you will learn

  • How current savings plus monthly contributions can compound over time.
  • How your projected pot grows across the years to retirement.
  • How a projection compares against a target you set.
  • Why planning — not children — is the dependable route to later-life security.

3. Beyond money

Security in later life is more than a number. Children are not a reliable retirement plan; deliberate planning is.

CommunityFriendships, neighbours, and chosen family — actively built over time. ServicesPensions, insurance, healthcare, and care services you arrange for yourself. HomeHousing that suits ageing, or plans to adapt or downsize. DocumentsA will, power of attorney, and care wishes recorded in advance.

Key takeaway

Later-life security comes from planning — savings, community, services, and clear documents — not from expecting children to provide it. Whatever your numbers, the dependable move is to plan deliberately and build a support network on purpose.

Pronatalist Phrase Translator

Society · Difficulty: Beginner · ~6 min

Common pressure phrases often carry a hidden assumption. This translator shows the assumption underneath a phrase — and a kind, clear reply. It's for understanding, not for catching anyone out; most of these come from people who mean well.

What you will learn

  • The assumption hiding inside familiar phrases.
  • How to answer the assumption, not just the words.
  • How naming it gently reduces its power over you.

Key takeaway

Behind most pressure is an assumption, and behind most assumptions is care expressed clumsily. Name the assumption gently, answer that, and you keep both your boundary and the relationship.

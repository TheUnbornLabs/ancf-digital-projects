PROJECT 067

“Auntie Uncle Chase” Arcade Game

Games · Difficulty: Beginner

A friendly arcade metaphor: gather "freedom tokens" while keeping your distance from the red "pressure" squares. No real people are targeted — the red squares are the questions, not anyone you love.

Controls: the arrow keys, or drag your finger across the game.

Collect tokens, avoid pressureFreedom tokens: 0

The idea behind it

The red squares drift toward you — slowly, persistently, with the best intentions, like a room full of well-meaning relatives. You can't make them stop; you just keep moving, collecting little wins of freedom, and not letting them corner you.

This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

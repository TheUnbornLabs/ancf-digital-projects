Video Script Generator

Scaffold a short, respectful explainer-video script — hook, body, tone, and a kind call-to-action. Pick a topic and length, generate, then make it your own.

3. Tips

Win the first 3 seconds

Open with the question your viewer already has. If the hook lands, the rest gets watched.

One idea, said plainly

Short videos can't argue everything. Pick one point and make it land.

End kind

Close by inviting people to decide for themselves and be gentle with others — it ages better than a hot take.

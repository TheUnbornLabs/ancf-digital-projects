# Reproductive Autonomy Explainer

*Project 006 · Category: Ethics · Difficulty: Intermediate*

A 360-degree interactive explainer on reproductive autonomy — the principle that each person
decides whether, when, and how to have children, including none. It is about *who decides*,
not *what they decide*.

Modules: what it is (with ICPD Cairo 1994 and reproductive-justice context); the six conditions
a free choice needs (interactive cards); how coercion runs in *both* directions (forced
sterilization and forced birth alike) with a short rights history; a personal autonomy
self-check; autonomy scenarios (doctor, partner, state); an autonomy-respecting response
generator; a quiz; and reflection.

Vanilla JS on the shared `../../ancf-ui.js` toolkit; verified with a headless DOM test (9/9).
Nothing is uploaded.

> This project is for education and reflection only. It is not medical, legal, financial, or
> mental health advice.

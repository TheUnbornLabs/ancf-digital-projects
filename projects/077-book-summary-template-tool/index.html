Book Summary Template Tool

Writing Tools · Difficulty: Beginner · ~15 min · template

A simple, reusable template for summarising any book you read — the thesis, key ideas, a few lines in your own words, your critique, and one takeaway. Build a live summary, copy it, and keep a small library of the books you've worked through.

Key takeaway

You don't really know what you think of a book until you've tried to summarise it. Naming the thesis, your honest critique, and one takeaway turns passive reading into understanding you keep. Reuse this for every book — your library becomes a map of your own thinking.

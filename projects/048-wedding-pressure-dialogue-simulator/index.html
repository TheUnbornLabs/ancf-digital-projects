Wedding Pressure Dialogue Simulator

Society · Difficulty: Intermediate · ~12 min · simulator

Weddings are peak season for "you're next!" This short, branching simulator lets you rehearse calm responses in a safe space, see how each choice tends to land over a three-round conversation, and track the balance of warmth and firmness in your replies. There's no "winning" — only finding what feels like you.

① The conversation② The three response styles③ Quick check④ Reflection

① The conversation

You're at a cousin's wedding. A well-meaning aunt sidles over… Pick a reply each round and see where it goes.

② The three response styles

Each reply leans one of three ways. None is "right" — they suit different people and moments. Tap to expand.

③ Quick check

Two quick questions — educational, not a test.

1. The aim of rehearsing these replies is to…
Feel calmer and more prepared, in your own voice
Memorise one perfect comeback
Win and embarrass the asker

2. A purely deflecting reply tends to…
Keep the peace now, but the question may return

④ Your reflection

Saved on this device only.

Key takeaway

There's no single correct way to answer "you're next!" — only the way that fits you and the moment. Deflecting keeps the peace, honesty plants a seed, and firmness draws a line. Most people end up using all three, and the more you rehearse, the more naturally the right one arrives when you need it.

PROJECT 089

Respectful Language Guide

Community Tools · Difficulty: Beginner

A practical guide to language that persuades without wounding.

Prefer 'I' and ideas

'I’ve chosen…' and 'This argument claims…' keep focus off attacking people.

Avoid dehumanising terms

Skip insults and group slurs entirely — they undermine your case and harm people.

Name behaviours, not identities

Critique 'this pressure' or 'this claim', not someone’s character or community.

Reflection prompt

Which phrase will you swap out next time?

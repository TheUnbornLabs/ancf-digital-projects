3. Tips

Earn the swipe

Slide 1 is the whole game — a clear hook that promises one useful thing. The rest delivers it.

One idea per slide

Carousels work because each slide is small. Resist cramming; let the swipe do the pacing.

Uplift, don't attack

Bust myths gently and avoid hashtags or lines that target any group. Respect ages better than outrage.
